# **App Name**: CardMatch

## Core Features:

- Digital Card Management: Allow users to add, view, edit, and delete digital client card records and their associated details.
- Physical Card Data Input: Provide an interface to manually enter details from physical client cards, such as ID or name, for comparison.
- Intelligent Matching Tool: Use an AI tool to intelligently suggest potential matches between physical card entries and digital records, even with slight discrepancies.
- Reconciliation Report: Display a clear report of matched, unmatched, and conflicting physical and digital card records, highlighting discrepancies.
- Zone-Based Data Grouping: Enable users to assign cards to specific zones and generate aggregated reports based on these zones, showing reconciliation status per zone.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A sophisticated, muted blue (#2E7BB2) to convey trust, clarity, and professionalism, suitable for a precise reconciliation tool. This choice avoids overly bold tones while remaining authoritative.
- Background color: A very light, almost white, subtle blue (#EDF5F8), sharing the same hue as the primary color but heavily desaturated to provide a clean and calming backdrop for data-heavy interfaces and reports.
- Accent color: A vibrant yet analogous aqua (#4FE0E0), used to highlight interactive elements, calls to action, and important data points within reports. Its brightness provides good contrast with the primary and background colors.
- All text: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for its modern, clean, and highly readable characteristics, making it ideal for presenting detailed client card data and reports with optimal clarity and neutrality across the application.
- Utilize a suite of simple, clean, and contemporary line-art icons that maintain visual consistency and clarity throughout the application, especially for actions like adding, editing, matching, and reporting.
- Implement a structured, grid-based layout for clear data presentation and intuitive navigation. Prioritize easy comparison of information, especially in reconciliation views and zone reports.
- Incorporate subtle, functional animations for filtering data, confirming actions, or expanding/collapsing sections. These should enhance the user experience without distracting from the data or feeling overly flashy, reinforcing precision.
